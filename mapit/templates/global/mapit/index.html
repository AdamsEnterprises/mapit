Mapit: Global

Mapit is a service that maps geographical points to administrative areas. It’s useful for anyone who needs to turn a longitude/latitude into the name or boundaries of a council, constituency, region, or more.

Non-commercial, low-volume use of this service is free – read the licence. You can also download the source on Github. Need a commercial licence? Get in touch.
